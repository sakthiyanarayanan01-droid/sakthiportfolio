Lumière Hair Studio — a booking-first site that fills the chair, not just the gallery.

Clienthair & beauty salon Rolefrontend developer Timeline3 weeks

Salon sites usually optimize for looking pretty. This one needed to optimize for getting booked — so the "Book now" action follows you down the page on every device.

the problem

Gorgeous Instagram reel, no way to book from it.

Lumière's audience found them through short-form video, tapped through to their old site, and bounced — there was a phone number buried in a "Contact" page and nothing else. Every missed booking was a lost client.

the approach

Make booking the obvious next step, everywhere.

Rather than a single contact form at the bottom, "Book now" is a persistent action — in the nav, after the gallery, after pricing, and pinned to the bottom of the screen on mobile. The gallery and service list are real content the salon can swap themselves.

  • Sticky mobile booking bar that appears after the first scroll
  • Service & pricing table that reads clearly on a small screen
  • Lightweight gallery grid using lazy-loaded, compressed images
the build

Bootstrap grid, custom everything else.

Used Bootstrap's grid system to move fast on layout, then layered custom CSS on top so it didn't look like a Bootstrap template. JavaScript handles the gallery lightbox and the sticky booking bar's show/hide behavior.
